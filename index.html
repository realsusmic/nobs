What is this

Any AI. Any chat.
Good answers don't need a four-paragraph runway.

By default, Claude, Gemini, Grok, ChatGPT — they all hedge, disclaim, restate your question, and wrap every answer in cotton wool before getting to the point.

It's not being careful. It's performing carefulness — optimized to seem thoughtful, not to be useful.

NoBS is a SKILL.md file you drop into any Claude Project. It eliminates the exact behaviors that waste your time, without changing anything else.

No jailbreaking. No hacks. Just a well-written instruction file that tells any AI to respect your time.
